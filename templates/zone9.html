Information on Planting Zone 9
USDA Plant Hardiness Zone 9 is considered a year-round planting zone. Located in California, Arizona, Texas, Florida and along the Gulf of Mexico coast, this zone features warm winters and hot summers. With an average minimum winter temperature of 20 to 30 degrees F, Zone 9 features active gardens throughout the entire year.

What plants can you grown in Zone 9?

Vegetables
Broccoli, Brussel Sprouts, Cabbage, Okra, Spinach

Fruits
Avocado, Hardy Kiwi, Olives, Passionfruit, Starfruit
