Information on Planting Zone 10
USDA Plant Hardiness Zone 10 is located in Southern inland California, southern Florida and Hawaii are the three small areas where the average minimum winter temperature only falls between 30 to 40 degrees F. The ability of Zone 10 gardeners to avoid freezing temperatures is a huge bonus for winter gardening, but the extreme heat of the summer months limits planting possibilities.

What plants can you grown in Zone 10?

Vegetables
Radish, Bitter Melon, Jicama, Peanuts, Tomatillos

Fruits
Apple Guava, Carica Papaya, Jackfruit, June Plum, Soursop
