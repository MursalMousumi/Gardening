Information on Planting Zone 6
USDA Hardiness Zone 6 covers a large portion of the United States. Known as a generally mild climate, the average minimum winter temperature is between -10 to 0 degrees F. With cold winter and mild-to-hot summers, you have many growing options in Zone 6. What plants can you grown in Zone 6?

Vegetables
Bush Beans, Butter Lettuce, Indeterminate Tomatoes, Longer Season Melons, Winter Squash

Fruits (Peach Varieties)
Jefferson, Late Crawford, Loring, Madison, Nectar, Red Globe
