Welcome to the home page of the gardening website

There are 12 Planting Zones in America. If you click on the Regions link above, it will take you to a page with information of the 12 Planting Zones. From there you can navigate to the individual Zone pages to find more information. There will be a list of vegetables and fruits that can be grown in the respective Zones. Within the pages, there will be links for more information on fruits and vegetables including a watering schedule.
