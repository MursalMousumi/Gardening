Information on Planting Zone 12
USDA Plant Hardiness Zone 12 and 13 are located in both Hawaii and Puerto Rico. These two plant hardiness zones are extremely warm, tropical environments that are best suited for plants tolerant of intense heat. With the average minimum winter temperature between 50 and 70 degrees F, Zones 12 and 13, the warmest of all the USDA hardiness zones, feature tropical plants and exotic fruits.

What plants can you grown in Zone 12 & 13?

Vegetables
Bush Beans, Eggplants, Hot Peppers, Summer Squash, Tomatoes

Fruits
African Apricot, Ackee, Bignay, Imbe, Black Pepper
