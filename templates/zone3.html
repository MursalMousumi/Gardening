Information on Planting Zone 3
Plant hardiness Zone 3 is found throughout Alaska, the northern portions of the United States and in high altitude areas. This zone features minimum average temperatures of -40 to -30 degrees F. Depending on the geographical location of your growing space, high winds, extreme cold and low moisture may affect growing conditions.

What plants can you grown in Zone 3?

Vegetables
Asparagus, Celery, Cucumbers, Summer Squash, Winter Squash

Fruits
Cupid cherry, Dolgo crabapple, Early gold pear, Golden Spice Pear, Goodland apple, Sweet Sixteen apple, Toka plum, Waneta plum, Westcot apricot

Herbs
Caraway, Catnip, English Chamomile, French sorrel, Garlic, Horseradish, Parsley, Peppermint
