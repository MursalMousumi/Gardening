Region 5

Temperature: Subzones inside this region can have temperatures ranging from -20 to -10 degrees F. Although still below the freezing point, these temperatures can still support plant life for fruits, vegetables and other plants that are very common. Growable Fruits and Vegetables: These include things like apples, peaches, pears and plums that are grown on trees. Leafy greens like kale and spinach, along with roots like radish.
